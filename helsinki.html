Helsinki

Tidal Club Helsinki is a weekly open and freeform workshop, where we practise live coding programs together. These different open source programs make it possible to quickly write code that produces improvised electronic music in a very intuitive way that differs largely from the traditional digital audio workstation programs.

The workshop will not have a teacher. Instead it’s an open space for learning and practising different aspects of the program together. Participating the workshop doesn’t require any previous knowledge about music making or programming. We strive to keep the workshop as easily approachable as possible and will assist any newcomers as best we can.

Every Tuesday at the new main library of Helsinki Oodi (Töölölahdenkatu 4) from 6PM until 9PM or 10 PM depending on participants. We will use the learning space 8 on the 2nd floor as our weekly meeting place.
